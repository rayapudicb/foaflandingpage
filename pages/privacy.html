Privacy

Private by default, with clearer local context.

nyr is designed so personal requests stay inside trusted circles, business content stays separate, and verification adds context without turning the product into a public feed. This page is the plain-English overview.

01

Private by default

Your profile, requests and local activity are built for controlled sharing, not open public discovery.

  • Relevant visibility instead of broad exposure
  • Context around who can respond
  • Less noise than public social feeds
02

Verified circles

Identity and work or college verification add trust context when people need it.

  • Direct circles first
  • Mutual connection context where available
  • Extra trust signals for work and campus circles
03

Separate business layer

Business posts, offers and campaigns are intentionally separated from person-to-person requests.

  • Verified local business presence
  • Cleaner request feed for people
  • Less spammy business discovery
