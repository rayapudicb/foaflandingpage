Circle business · Promotions

Run promotions that respect trust, consent, and local rules

Keep promotions inside trusted-circle boundaries—no public ads, no cold reach.

Back to home FAQ

Promotion basics

  • Target only Level 1/2; no exceptions.
  • Set clear start/end dates and limits.
  • Keep it relevant to local context and laws.

Consent & control

  • Easy mute/unfollow for any promotion.
  • No surprise notifications; respect user settings.
  • Honor redemption terms exactly as stated.

Prohibited behaviors

  • Cold DMs, mass outreach, or public ads.
  • Unclear pricing or hidden conditions.
  • Sharing user data outside the trusted-circle context.

Compliance & review

  • Follow local promo rules (age, disclosures, taxes).
  • Trust & Safety may review or remove non-compliant promos.
  • Report problematic promotions directly from the card.
