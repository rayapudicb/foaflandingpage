Clear rules for a higher-trust local network.

This is the plain-English overview of how nyr and nyr Business are expected to work. Use real information where required, respect privacy, keep posts accurate, and do not treat the product like a public spam channel.

01

For people using nyr

  • Post real requests, offers and recommendations in the right category.
  • Do not impersonate, scam, harass or misuse trusted-circle context.
  • Share only information you are comfortable sharing with the audience you choose.
  • Follow local laws and community standards.
02

For businesses using nyr Business

  • Represent a real local business and keep business details accurate.
  • Use offers, campaigns and chats responsibly and transparently.
  • Do not mislead people with false claims, fake urgency or spammy posting.
  • Respect the separation between business content and person-to-person requests.
03

Verification and trust

  • Verification adds trust context but does not guarantee every interaction.
  • We may review or remove accounts, posts or business access that abuse the system.
  • Additional verification may be requested when safety or fraud concerns appear.
04

Questions or disputes

  • Use support for access issues, moderation disputes, privacy requests or business onboarding questions.
  • Account deletion requests should use the dedicated delete-account route.
  • Privacy questions should go through the privacy support channel.
