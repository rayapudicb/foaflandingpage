Quick answers

Privacy-first, spam-free, people you actually know.

How do you read my contacts?

With permission, once. Data is used to detect real relationships—never sold, never public.

What stops spam?

No one outside Level 1 or Level 2 can reach you. Businesses live in their own tab.

Is anything public?

No. Only Level 1 and Level 2 can see or post to you. Nothing is public or searchable.

What about emergencies?

One tap alerts Level 1 contacts nearest to you. Location is shared only with them.
