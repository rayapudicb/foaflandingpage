How Nyr works

Three things people need to understand in 20 seconds.

Nyr is simple in plain English: real people first, trust levels next, clear post types after that.

01

Verify real people

Everyone starts with identity verification. That keeps fake profiles, cold-start spam and anonymous posting out of the feed.

ID check No public profile Spam blocked

Result: the network begins with real identities, not random accounts.

02

Trust levels stay meaningful

Contacts are organized into Level 1 and Level 2. You confirm who you truly know, so every introduction still carries context.

Level 1 Level 2 Mutual context

Result: people do not appear as strangers just because they are nearby.

03

Posts are easy to read

Help, rides, item loans, recommendations, emergencies and circle offers each use a clear card, so intent is obvious at a glance.

Result: the feed feels useful fast instead of messy or ambiguous.

Quick answers

What people ask before they join.

The original FAQ stays, but the cards now sit inside the same visual language as the rest of the page.

How do you read my contacts?

With permission, once. Data is used to detect real relationships, not to publish your graph or sell it.

What stops spam?

No one outside Level 1 or Level 2 can reach you. Businesses stay in their own tab and do not boost into the main feed.

Is anything public?

No. Only Level 1 and Level 2 can see or post to you. Nothing is public or searchable on the open web.

What about emergencies?

One tap alerts Level 1 contacts nearest to you. Location is shared only with them, only for that moment.
